How Merritt Saves You

    Food Ordering

    Each week, Merritt orders all the food for the Top's 5 food-selling businesses plus citrus and smallwares for its other 3 bar businesses.

    Catering

    For the Top's $xx/year catering business, Merritt:

    • Creates menus
    • Prepares bespoke tastings for clients
    • Orders food quantities appropriate to the guest count
    • Preps food to be cooked
    • Cooks all food for every function, with guest counts ranging from 15 - 300 people
    • Delivers catered food to Depot Event Space
    • Creates special experiences for guests such as cooked-to-order grilled cheese bar
